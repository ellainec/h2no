A time not to distant in the future, the era of the Great Disaster, where humans had completely wasted the limited fresh water resource they had been gifted on earth. A young scientist going by the name of Marina, built a robot named H2-NO. She had managed to build a time travel machine. and wanting to stop the Era of the Great Disaster from occuring, Marina sent H2-NO back in time to save the future of the world, by attempting to build up awareness in the children of Vancouver about water waste! Your job is to help H2-NO turn off the major water wasting appliances around the city of Vancouver!
