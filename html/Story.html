H2NO
A time not too distant in the future, humans have completely wasted the limited supply of fresh water on earth. As a final attempt to save the world, a bright young scientist builds a super robot . This robot, H2-NO, is sent back in time to present day Vancouver and it's designed to educate and build awareness about water conservation and fight water waste. Your job is to help guild H2-NO through the city of Vancouver, but be careful! H2-NO wasn't made waterproof.
